## Why This Figure Matters
Bret Victor appears as a recurring anchor for humane interfaces, dynamic representation, explorable explanation, and the relationship between medium and thought. He is likely to connect multiple future threads across software tools, learning design, embodiment, and epistemology.

## What They Represent
A designer-thinker arguing that interfaces, representations, and media shape what humans are able to think. He represents a push away from passive symbolic consumption and toward dynamic, explorable, more embodied forms of understanding.

## Signature Ideas
- Explorable explanations and dynamic media
- Humane representation of thought
- Tools that let people see and manipulate behavior directly

## Core Distinctions
- Representation is not neutral; it enables or suppresses latent capabilities
- Dynamic, explorable models versus static symbolic description
- Authoring for human understanding versus engineering for machine reliability
